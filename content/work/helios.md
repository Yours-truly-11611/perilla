Helios Group backs early-stage climate technology companies. By 2023 they had a portfolio of fourteen businesses across energy storage, carbon removal, sustainable agriculture, and ocean health — and a website that hadn't kept pace with the ambition of the work.

We refreshed their brand to project the confidence of a group that has moved beyond advocacy into execution. The new visual language is precise and direct: no green gradients, no leaf icons, no performative gestures. These people are building infrastructure. The brand should feel like it.

The web platform we designed serves a dual audience — the LPs who fund the group, and the founders seeking investment. Both need different things from the same site. We resolved this through architecture rather than separate destinations: a single, fluid experience that reveals depth as users move through it.
