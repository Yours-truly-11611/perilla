Meridian Spirits distil single-origin whisky in the Scottish Borders using methods unchanged since 1887. They had a product of genuine distinction and packaging that honoured it. What they lacked was motion.

For the launch campaign, we directed and produced a three-minute brand film shot over four days across the distillery, the surrounding landscape, and the cellars where the casks rest for twelve years. We wanted to make time feel like the protagonist — because for a craft spirit, time is the product.

The motion system we developed extends the film's visual language across social, events, and trade. Slow reveals, grain overlays, a colour grade pulled from the spirit itself. Nothing moves faster than it needs to.

The film has since been screened at three international spirits competitions and won a bronze at the Cannes Lions Health & Wellness (in the adjacent category). More importantly, Meridian sold out their first UK allocation in eleven days.
