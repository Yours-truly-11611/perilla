Aurelius Financial came to us with a problem familiar to many in their sector: a brand that looked like every other wealth management firm. Muted blues, generic serif, stock photography. Safe to the point of invisible.

We spent three weeks in research before touching a sketchbook — interviewing partners, auditing competitors, and mapping the emotional territory they wanted to own. The word that kept surfacing was *restraint*. Their clients don't want flash. They want confidence expressed through precision.

The identity we built centres on a custom wordmark drawn from classical proportions, paired with a typographic system that rewards close reading. The palette is deliberate: near-black, warm white, and a single accent drawn from aged parchment. Every touchpoint — from letterhead to client portal — reinforces the same quiet conviction.

> "We've had three clients mention our new cards in meetings. That never happened before."
> — James Aurelius, Managing Partner
