Carta Nova publishes works in translation that deserve a wider audience than they'd otherwise find — literature from languages and regions chronically underrepresented in the English-speaking world.

The identity had to carry two things simultaneously: the weight of literary seriousness, and the warmth of genuine curiosity. We landed on a mark derived from early cartographic lettering — the carta of the name — paired with a typographic system flexible enough to serve a wide range of cover treatments while maintaining family coherence.

The book design system is the project we're most proud of in this engagement: a set of rules and variables that allows their in-house designer to produce covers that feel considered and distinctive without requiring external art direction on every title.
