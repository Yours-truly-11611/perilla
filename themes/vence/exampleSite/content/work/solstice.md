The Solstice Collective is six architects who left established firms to build something different — a practice committed to sustainable residential design that doesn't apologise for being beautiful.

They needed a digital presence that matched their ambition without the usual architecture-firm clichés: no full-bleed drone shots, no grid of identical project thumbnails, no impenetrable Helvetica.

We designed a site that moves the way their buildings feel — unhurried, generous with space, attentive to light. The scroll experience was choreographed project-by-project, with each piece of work given room to breathe. Photography direction kept human presence central; these are homes, not objects.

The result is one of our favourite things we've made: a site that people spend time with rather than skim.
